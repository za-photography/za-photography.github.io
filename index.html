About


A self-taught mid-20's creative (she/her) currently living in Manchester.

My practice takes influence from the every day, mundanity of life & forgotten aspects of our daily lives.
My exploration started during my time at university whilst reading critical & cultural theories including psychogeography & the rapid urbanisation & gentrification of my birth city as starting point.
My perspective & practice is ever-changing & evolving as I encounter new experiences/situations through life. We are always evolving & on the go even when we don't think we are.
My journey into photography has led to developing skills in social media/marketing, journalism, project management & working with diverse communities through paid & voluntary projects.

This website represents my practice & includes humans, objects, landscape & animals as my subjects.
